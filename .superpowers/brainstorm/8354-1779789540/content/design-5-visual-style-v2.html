Design Principles

Scholarly restraint
No bright colors or flashy effects. Muted earth tones. Typography does the heavy lifting. Italic serif for opening questions — like a journal epigraph.
Evidence-forward
Statistical details shown naturally (β, p-values, n). Figures presented in clean white frames like journal exhibits. Key points read like findings, not marketing bullets.
Quiet authority
Thin rules and small caps instead of badges. Generous whitespace. Animations are gentle fades, not flashy entrances. The content speaks — the design recedes.

Typography
Headlines: Georgia, regular weight (not bold)
Hook question: Georgia italic
Body/points: System sans-serif, light weight
Labels: Sans-serif small caps, wide tracking
Brand: Georgia small caps, very subtle
Animation
Gentle fades (600-800ms, ease-out)
Minimal movement (20-30px max offset)
No spring physics — linear or ease curves
Key points: staggered fade, 200ms delay each
