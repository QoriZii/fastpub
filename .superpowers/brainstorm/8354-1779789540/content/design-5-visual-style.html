Design Tokens

Colors (Solarized)
#073642 — dark background (hook, closing)
#fdf6e3 — light background (content scenes)
#268bd2 — approach accent (blue)
#b58900 — results accent (yellow)
#dc322f — problem accent (red)
#6c71c4 — hook accent (violet)
#d33682 — significance accent (magenta)
Typography
Headlines: Georgia serif, 700 weight
Body: System sans-serif (-apple-system)
Labels: Consolas monospace, uppercase, tracked
Key points: Left border accent + sans body
Layout Rules
Dark bg: hook + closing (bookend scenes)
Light bg: all content scenes
Split layout: text left (~55%), figure right (~45%)
Brand mark: "fastpub" monospace, bottom-left, subtle
Does this visual direction feel right? Solarized palette, warm academic aesthetic, matching ytlang-web's DNA.
