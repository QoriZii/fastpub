What Changed

Warm gold accent
A gold-brown accent thread runs through every scene — gradient bars, border accents, brand mark. Adds warmth and visual continuity without being loud.
Gradient depth
Dark scenes use subtle gradients instead of flat color. Geometric circle accents add visual interest. Content scenes get a gradient top bar for scene-type identity.
Bolder typography
Headlines are now bold (700). Key points have subtle background tints behind left borders. Scene type labels use color. Still readable, but more presence.
